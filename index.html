OoruLabs | Talking Heads

Dr Ruth Oldenziel

Professor in the History of Technology at Eindhoven University of Technology. With PhD at Yale she is the Editor in Chief of the Journal of Technology and Culture, Johns Hopkins University.

Dr Paul Barter

He is a transport-policy researcher, adviser, writer and trainer with a background in academia and in non-profits. He focuses on urban transport policy and parking

Dirk de Jager

He has experience as a city councilor in various city districts in Amsterdam. He has experience with vision development, strategy and design of bicycle infrastructure.

Dr Ashwin Mahesh

He is a scientist, an urbanist, journalist, politician and social technologist. He is working to increase the number of problem solving people.

Ravichandar V

Urban and Civic evangelist, Member Bengaluru Restructuring Committee, Designer of the Bengaluru property tax Self Assessment Scheme, Evangelist JNNURM.

Dr Anjali K Mohan

She has over three decades of experience in development, institutional and policy frameworks, urban planning and management with work in many cities across India.

Vinayak Bharne

A Presidential Fellow of USC Marshall School of Business. Faculty, USC School of Architecture, Price School of Public Policy, East Asian Studies Center, US-China Institute, among others

Vishwanath S

AKA Zenrainman, he has been the guru on water for over four decades. Beyond helping people with water sustainability measures he has been an mentor and a teacher for many in the city.

Naresh Narasimhan

Runs a successful architecture firm, but has created more impact in the city by his work outside the building with projects like Church street and the K100 waterways project.

Shreya Gadepalli

Founder of Urbanworks Institute and ITDP India practice she guided dozens of cities to design and implement public transport systems and inclusive streets.

Alok Prasanna Kumar

He is Co-Founder and Lead, Vidhi Karnataka. His areas of research include judicial reforms, Constitutional law, urban development, and law and technology.

Satya Arikutharam

He recently advised a state government in India and provided key inputs to the Unified Metropolitan Transport Authority in Bengaluru and to promote active mobility in Karnataka.

Ravi Gadepalli

He works on projects advancing bus systems, paratransit services and electric mobility. He works with the World Bank, Asian Development Bank, UITP and IFC

Srinivas Alavilli

He is a strong believer in systems like ward committees that enable citizen participation and bridge the gap between politicians and citizens in local governance.

Amit Gupta

Co-Founder and CEO of Yulu, a leading micromobility shared services company in India. He was earlier the co-founder of InMobi a successful startup.

Arjit Soni

A chartered accountant and the founder of one of Indias few functional multicity bicycle sharing company, MY-BYK
