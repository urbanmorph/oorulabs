Featured Guests

OoruLabs | Talking Heads

Dr Ashwin Mahesh

He is a scientist, an urbanist, journalist, politician and social technologist. He is working to increase the number of problem solving people.

Dirk de Jager

He has experience as a city councilor in various city districts in Amsterdam. He has experience with vision development, strategy and design of bicycle infrastructure.

Dr Paul Barter

He is a transport-policy researcher, adviser, writer and trainer with a background in academia and in non-profits. He focuses on urban transport policy and parking

Dr Anjali K Mohan

She has over three decades of experience in development, institutional and policy frameworks, urban planning and management with work in Bhopal, Ranchi, Dharwad, Delhi and Bangalore.

Shreya Gadepalli

Founder of Urbanworks Institute and ITDP India practice she guided dozens of cities to design and implement public transport systems and inclusive streets, as well as regulate motor vehicle use.

Alok Prasanna Kumar

Alok Prasanna Kumar is Co-Founder and Lead, Vidhi Karnataka. His areas of research include judicial reforms, Constitutional law, urban development, and law and technology.

Satya Arikutharam

He recently advised a state government in India and provided key inputs to the Unified Metropolitan Transport Authority in Bengaluru and to promote active mobility in the state of Karnataka, India

Ravi Gadepalli

He works on projects advancing bus systems, paratransit services and electric mobility. He works with the World Bank, Asian Development Bank, UITP and IFC

Srinivas Alavilli

Srinivas is a strong believer in systems like ward committees that enable citizen participation and bridge the gap between politicians and citizens in local governance.

Amit Gupta

Co-Founder and CEO of Yulu, a leading micromobility shared services company in India. He was earlier the co-founder of InMobi a successful startup.

Arjit Soni

A chartered accountant and the founder of one of Indias few functional multicity bicycle sharing company, MY-BYK
